Discover Abadan

Abadan is a beautiful city located in southwestern Iran, known for its rich history, oil refinery, and cultural heritage. It is situated on Abadan Island in the Arvand River.

×

History of Abadan

Abadan has a deep and complex history, dating back to ancient times. It became particularly significant in the 20th century due to its oil refinery, one of the largest in the world. Abadan played a crucial role during the Iran-Iraq war and is known for its resilience and strategic importance.

Places to Visit in Abadan

  • Abadan Oil Refinery
  • Minoo Island
  • Abadan Museum
  • Palace of Culture and Art

Where is Abadan?

Abadan is located in southwestern Iran, near the border with Iraq. It is on Abadan Island in the Arvand River.
